The person behind the posts

Honest writing from someone still figuring it out

I'm John — 18 years old, based in Livingston, Scotland. I'm finishing an HNC in Engineering Systems and I hold a Mitsubishi Electric Ecodan heat pump certification. I'm figuring out the same things most people my age are — money, career, fitness, and what it all means.

Unfiltered started because I got tired of reading content that was either too polished to be real, or too vague to be useful. I wanted a place to write the stuff I actually think — about investing when you're young and skint, about getting fit without losing your head, about working in engineering when the world is shifting fast.

Nothing here is sponsored. Nothing is filtered through a PR team. It's just me, writing what I know and what I'm learning. Some of it will age well. Some of it probably won't. That's kind of the point.
